PEBC Blueprint Update - Platform Changes Coming February 24, 2026

Published: January 2026

Key Information

  • Platform Update Date: February 24, 2026
  • First Affected Exam: May 2026 PEBC Qualifying Examination
  • February 2026 Candidates: No changes to your preparation

Blueprint Update Announcement

We are aware of the recently released updates to the PEBC Pharmacist Qualifying Examination blueprint and are actively working to ensure our platform aligns fully with the revised content requirements.

Our Commitment to February 2026 Candidates

To avoid any disruption during this critical preparation period, we will not implement changes immediately. Maintaining consistency and stability for February exam candidates is our priority—we will not alter question distribution or study flow until after your examination.

For February 2026 Candidates

If you are preparing for the February 2026 examination, you can continue your studies without any concerns. Question distribution and study flow will remain unchanged until after your exam.

Implementation Timeline

The full platform update will be implemented on February 24, 2026, immediately after the February examination window concludes. The first PEBC Qualifying Examination affected by the new blueprint will be the May 2026 sitting. Candidates preparing for May 2026 and beyond can be assured that our platform will be fully updated well in advance.

What This Means for You

  • February 2026 Candidates: Continue your preparation as planned—no changes will affect you
  • May 2026 and Beyond: Platform will be fully updated by February 24, 2026, giving you ample time to prepare
  • All Users: We will communicate additional details as we approach the implementation date

Final Note

We appreciate your trust in passMCQ. Our team is committed to providing the most up-to-date and relevant preparation materials for your success.

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about this update or how it affects your preparation, please don't hesitate to contact our support team.
