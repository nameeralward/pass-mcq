This Is a Big One

We’re thrilled to announce one of the largest single content updates in passMCQ history: over 800 new practice questions have been added to the platform today. This isn’t a small refresh—it’s a major expansion of your question bank, built from the ground up to match the current exam landscape.

Every one of these questions has been written and reviewed to align with the latest Pharmacist Qualifying Examination guidelines and competency framework. That means you’re practicing with content that reflects how the exam is structured today—including the updated blueprint, competency areas, and emphasis on entry-to-practice skills. After we updated the platform to the new 2026 blueprint, we didn’t stop there. We doubled down on content so that your practice bank grows with the new structure.
