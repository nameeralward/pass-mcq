Payment Update

Pay in Installments with Affirm & Klarna — Now Available on passMCQ!

Flexible payment options so nothing stands between you and your exam success.

Publication Date: March 2026

We know that investing in your PEBC Qualifying Examination preparation is one of the most important steps in your pharmacy career — and we want to make sure cost is never a barrier. That's why we're happy to announce that passMCQ now offers installment payment plans through Affirm and Klarna, two of the world's most trusted buy-now-pay-later platforms.

Simply open our web app and try to buy a subscription — you'll see all the options right there. Choose Affirm or Klarna at checkout and pick the schedule that works for you. No need to contact support first; everything is built into the app.

affirm

Simple, transparent installment plans with no hidden fees.

klarna

Smooth, flexible payments trusted by millions worldwide.

Available Installment Schedules

When you subscribe in the app, you can choose from three schedules:

6
Weeks

Pay it off quickly — ideal if you have an exam coming up soon and want the shortest commitment.

6
Months

A balanced plan — spread the cost over half a year while focusing fully on your studies.

12
Months

The most flexible option — the lowest installments spread across a full year.

How It Works

  1. Open our web app – Go to app.pass-mcq.ca and sign in or create an account.
  2. Go to subscribe – When you're ready, start the process to buy a subscription. You'll see all available plans and payment options.
  3. Choose Affirm or Klarna at checkout – Select your preferred schedule (6 weeks, 6 months, or 12 months) and complete the flow with Affirm or Klarna.
  4. Get instant access – Once your first payment is confirmed, your subscription is activated right away. Start studying immediately — no waiting.

Why We're Offering This

At passMCQ, our mission has always been to give every pharmacist candidate in Canada the best possible chance at passing the PEBC Qualifying Examination. We heard from many of you that while the value of our platform was clear, the upfront cost of a full subscription could be difficult to manage — especially when you're juggling other expenses during your preparation period.

By offering installment invoicing through Affirm and Klarna, we're removing that financial friction. Your exam success matters more than the payment schedule — and now you can access our full suite of practice questions, study tools, and features without having to pay everything at once.
