VERSION 2.0

Exciting News! We've Enhanced Our Platform with Amazing New Features!

Discover what's new and take your preparation to the next level!

Publication Date: December 2025

We're thrilled to announce passMCQ Version 2.0—a major update that transforms how you prepare for the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1. This release introduces three groundbreaking features designed to make your study experience more collaborative, focused, and supported than ever before.

Study Together

Studying for pharmacy exams can be isolating—but it doesn't have to be. StudyTogether is a revolutionary collaborative study feature that marks a first in Canada's PEBC exam preparation landscape.

  • Call with friends to study together in real-time – Connect instantly with study partners through integrated voice calls
  • Join call rooms with up to 4 people to discuss questions – The optimal group size for effective collaboration
  • Solve quiz questions together while on a call – All participants see the same question at the same time
  • Share your screen and walk through solutions together – Explain your thought process and learn from different perspectives
  • Get instant help from study partners when you're stuck – No more spending hours on a single problem

Did you know? Research shows that students retain 50-90% more information when they learn through discussion and teaching compared to passive reading. This is crucial for the PEBC exam, which tests comprehensive knowledge across multiple pharmacy domains.

A Canadian First

StudyTogether is the first integrated collaborative study feature specifically designed for PEBC exam preparation in Canada. Unlike other platforms that require separate apps for communication and studying, everything happens in one seamless experience.

No need for Zoom, Discord, or other third-party tools—everything is built right in!

How StudyTogether Works

  1. Create or Join a Study Session – Navigate to StudyTogether, create a new session or join using a code
  2. Enter the Call Room – Start a voice call with one tap—no app switching required
  3. Start Solving Questions Together – Select a quiz set and work through questions as a team
  4. Discuss and Learn – Share your reasoning, ask questions, and review answers together

Play Music

Create the perfect study atmosphere with our integrated music feature, designed to enhance your focus and cognitive performance.

  • Boost concentration with scientifically proven study music – Carefully selected tracks optimized for learning
  • Enhance focus with curated classical playlists – Experience the "Mozart Effect" during your study sessions
  • Improve memory and cognitive performance – Music at the right tempo enhances brain function
  • Reduce distractions and create the perfect study atmosphere – Block out environmental noise
  • Access premium playlists designed for optimal learning – No need for separate music apps

Communications

Stay connected and supported throughout your exam preparation journey with our enhanced communications system.

  • Get instant responses from our expert team – Quick answers to your questions
  • Stay informed with important updates and announcements – Never miss critical information
  • Receive personalized help when you need it most – Tailored support for your needs
  • Submit questions and get detailed explanations – Clear up any confusion
  • Connect directly with the passMCQ support team – We're here to help you succeed

These new features are available now for all passMCQ subscribers!
