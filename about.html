About Us

Our journey began in 2021 when a group of passionate pharmacists and tech enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to revolutionize how pharmacists prepare for the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1. Having experienced the challenges of exam preparation firsthand, we understood the need for a more intelligent, adaptive, and comprehensive study solution.

What started as a simple question bank has evolved into a sophisticated learning platform, incorporating cutting-edge AI technology and detailed analytics. Over the past three years, we've continuously refined our content and features based on user feedback and the latest developments in clinical therapeutics and Canadian practice guidelines.

Today, passMCQ represents the culmination of countless hours of development, testing, and improvement. We're proud to have helped numerous pharmacy professionals achieve success on the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1, and we remain committed to our mission of making high-quality exam preparation accessible to all.
