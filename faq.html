Frequently Asked Questions

What is passMCQ?

passMCQ is a comprehensive study companion app designed specifically for pharmacy professionals preparing for the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1 (MCQ). It offers over 4,000 practice questions, AI-powered assistance, and various study modes to optimize your exam preparation.

How do I purchase a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Simply download the app and select your preferred access plan within the app. All payments are processed securely through Apple's payment system.

Can I use passMCQ on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use passMCQ on multiple Apple devices with the same Apple ID. However, to prevent account sharing, we implement a device switching cooldown system. Please refer to our Device Switching Policy for details.

Is my progress saved across devices?

Yes, all your progress, bookmarks, and study data are synchronized across your devices when you're signed in with the same account.

How often is the content updated?

We regularly update our question bank and content to ensure accuracy and relevance. Updates are automatically delivered through the App Store.

What happens when my subscription expires?

When your subscription expires, you'll maintain access to your progress data but won't be able to access practice questions or study materials until you renew your subscription.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

How does the AI assistance work?

Our AI system analyzes your performance and study patterns to provide personalized explanations, identify knowledge gaps, and suggest focused study areas. It also helps generate detailed answer rationales and summaries for therapeutic topics.

Is passMCQ available on Android devices?

Currently, passMCQ is exclusively available for Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, and Mac). However, you can also access our platform through your web browser at app.pass-mcq.ca for a convenient alternative.

What makes the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1 challenging?

The PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1 (MCQ) is challenging because it:

  • Tests comprehensive knowledge across all therapeutic areas
  • Requires application of clinical knowledge to patient scenarios
  • Includes questions on Canadian pharmacy practice and regulations
  • Tests critical thinking and decision-making skills
  • Covers both theoretical knowledge and practical application

Our extensive question bank helps you practice with realistic exam-style questions to build confidence and competency.

How does passMCQ help with exam preparation?

passMCQ provides comprehensive preparation tools including:

  • Over 4,000 practice questions covering all therapeutic areas
  • Detailed explanations for each answer choice
  • AI-powered personalized assistance
  • Performance tracking and analytics
  • Mock exams with timer functionality
  • Study mode for self-paced learning
  • Bookmarking and review features
  • Progress synchronization across devices

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Refund policies are managed by Apple through the App Store. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a refund directly through Apple's refund process. Contact Apple Support or visit their refund request page to initiate the process.

How long should I study for the PEBC Qualifying Examination Part 1?

The recommended study duration varies based on your background and experience, but typically:

  • Recent pharmacy graduates: 2-3 months of dedicated study
  • Experienced pharmacists: 1-2 months of focused preparation
  • Those needing comprehensive review: 3-6 months

Our app includes progress tracking to help you gauge your readiness and adjust your study timeline accordingly.
